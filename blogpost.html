Own Your Morning

By Muthomi Pius | July 2, 2025

Take good care of the front end of your day, and the rest will take care of itself!

"We will have eternity to celebrate our victories, but only a few hours before sunset to win them." ~ Amy Carmichael

We often underestimate the power of the first hour of our day. But as Robin Sharma emphasizes in The 5 AM Club, how you start your morning determines how you live your day—and ultimately, your life.

Owning your morning doesn’t mean simply waking up early. It means using that sacred time with intention. It’s about setting a positive tone before the world starts demanding your attention. In the quiet of the early morning, you create space to center yourself, sharpen your focus, and fuel your growth.

One of Sharma’s core teachings is the 20/20/20 Formula: 20 minutes of movement, 20 minutes of reflection, and 20 minutes of learning. It’s a simple yet powerful routine that energizes the body, calms the mind, and nurtures the soul.

"Self education is, I believe, the only education there is." ~ Isaac Asimov

Start by moving your body—go for a jog, stretch, or do a quick workout. This releases dopamine and gets your blood flowing. Then, reflect. Meditate, journal, or pray. This clears mental clutter and connects you to your deeper purpose. Finally, learn—read, listen to a podcast, or study something new. This feeds your mind and keeps you growing.

When you consistently own your morning, you gain control over your mindset and your schedule. You show up with more clarity, creativity, and confidence. Over time, this transforms not just your productivity, but your entire life.

"The men who are great live with that which is substantial, not that which is superficial; they abide with realities , they remain not with that is showy. The one they discard, the other they hold" ~ Classic of the Way and Virtue by Lao Tzu

Remember, greatness is not a function of luck—it's the result of daily choices. Wake up with purpose, and the rest of your day will follow.

Own your morning. Elevate your life.
